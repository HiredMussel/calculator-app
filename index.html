Loan Calculator

For those who don't want to pay the cost of the course up front, there is the possibility of an interest-free loan from EdAid to cover some or all of the cost of the course.

You may borrow any amount up to the total cost of the course (£8,000). EdAid charge a 5% administrative fee which will be added to the total amount you repay.

In addition, an extra fee is charged if you borrow more than a certain amount. If you intend to borrow more than 80% of the course's cost (£6,400), then the total cost of the course is increased to £8,500.

If you borrow over 90% of the cost of the course (£7,200), then the total cost of the course is £9,000.

Repayments are made as a percentage of annual salary. At least 10% of your monthly salary must go towards repaying the cost of the course, starting from three months after your graduation. There is no minimum income threshold before this applies.

Payments are made on the 1st of each month, and there is no maximum number of months for a student to pay back the cost of the course.
